# TerraDash: 48-Hour Garden Fulfillment Strategy (Austin Launch)

**TL;DR**
TerraDash eliminates the 7.4-month design-to-dirt lag by offering standardized premium garden kits installed in a guaranteed 48-hour window. By leveraging a nursery-free logistics layer and W2-employed Garden Squads, we capture the "Move-In Max" market that prioritizes speed and reliability over bespoke complexity.

## 1. The 48-Hour Fulfillment Model
The "Weekend Sprint" is our core product. We achieve this through radical standardization of materials and a synchronized 2-day workflow.

| Timeframe | Activity | Key Deliverable |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pre-Arrival** | Logistics sync with local nursery; pre-pulling inventory. | Materials staged for 6:00 AM delivery. |
| **Day 1: AM** | Site clearing, soil amendment, and smart irrigation trenching. | Clean slate; irrigation backbone installed. |
| **Day 1: PM** | Hardscaping (gravel/pathways) and large specimen placement. | Structural layout complete. |
| **Day 2: AM** | Planting of "Garden Kit" palettes (15-30 gallon stock). | Instant-impact verticality and color. |
| **Day 2: PM** | Mulching, smart controller calibration, and client walkthrough. | Fully functional, automated garden. |

## 2. Logistics Strategy: The Nursery-Free Layer
TerraDash does not own land or inventory. We operate as a high-volume "Preferred Buyer" for established local nurseries.

### Partner Vetting Criteria (Austin/Phoenix)
We have identified the following partners for the Q3 launch:
- **Austin, TX**: Leaf Landscape Supply, Barton Springs Nursery, and The Great Outdoors.
- **Phoenix, AZ**: SummerWinds Nursery and Moon Valley Nurseries.

### Inventory Management
- **The "Pre-Pull" Agreement**: Partners receive our 6-week rolling forecast. In exchange for a 15% volume discount, they reserve "Kit-Standard" species (e.g., Agave Victoria-reginae for Desert Modernism).
- **Quality Control**: A TerraDash "Kit Lead" inspects all stock 24 hours before the install. If a specimen doesn't meet the "Impact Standard," the partner swaps it from their general inventory immediately.

## 4. 90-Day Launch Roadmap (Austin Test Region)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)
- **Inventory Lock**: Finalize SKU lists for the three regional kits (Pollinator, Desert, Lemonading) with Leaf Landscape Supply.
- **Hiring**: Recruit 2 "Founding Squad Leads" from high-end Austin design-build firms.
- **Tech Stack**: Deploy the scheduling engine that syncs nursery inventory levels with customer booking windows.

### Phase 2: The Beta Sprint (Days 31-60)
- **The "First Five"**: Execute 5 installs at a 20% discount for early adopters in the Mueller and Easton Park communities.
- **Content Engine**: Capture "Dirt-to-Garden" 48-hour time-lapses for social proof.
- **Calibration**: Adjust labor allocations based on real-world Austin soil (caliche rock) performance.

### Phase 3: GA Rollout (Days 61-90)
- **Full Capacity**: Scale to 4 Garden Squads (8 projects per weekend capacity).
- **Referral Loop**: Launch the "Neighbor-to-Neighbor" credit ($500 for successful referral in the same development).
- **Expansion Planning**: Scout nursery partners in Gilbert/Chandler, AZ for the Phoenix launch.

## 5. Strategic Moat: Smart Irrigation as Warranty
The standard inclusion of smart irrigation (e.g., Rachio 3) is our primary risk mitigation strategy.
- **Remote Monitoring**: TerraDash monitors system health for the first 90 days.
- **The "Mortality" Hedge**: We offer a 1-year plant guarantee *only* if the smart irrigation remains connected and active. This shifts the burden of care from the "black thumb" homeowner to our automated system.
